Getting Here

The wedding ceremony and reception are being held at Denton Hall, just a 5 minute drive from Ilkley.

If you are traveling by car, there is a free car park in the grounds at Denton Hall. It is very well sign posted on arrival.

For those traveling by train, we suggest you get off at Ilkley Station and travel by taxi to the venue.

Ilkley Taxis: 01943 603 330

Uber also operates in Ilkley.
